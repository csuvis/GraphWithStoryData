This case describes the friendship network between members of the Zachary's karate club. Nodes represent the club members and edges represent the friendship between members.
There was a conflict between the club president, John A., and the club instructor, Mr. Hi over the price of karate lessons. Mr. Hi, who wished to raise prices, claimed the authority to set his own lesson fees. But John A., who wished to stabilize prices. The officers, led by John A., fired Mr. Hi. The supporters of Mr. Hi retaliated by resigning and forming a new organization headed by Mr. Hi, thus completing the fission of the club.
