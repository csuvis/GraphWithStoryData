This case describes the relationship network of characters in Game of Thrones. Nodes represent the characters. Edges represent the relationships between the characters.
The narrative starts at a time of peace, with all the houses unified under the rule of King Robert Baratheon, who holds the Iron Throne. Early on, King Robert dies in a hunting accident. As a result, the war for the Iron Throne breaks out. Driven by cause or circumstance, the characters from the houses launch into arduous and intertwined journeys. Powerful houses mainly include the honorable Stark family, the pompous Lannisters, the slighted Baratheons, and the exiled Daenerys.
